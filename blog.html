Free time

Both the setting sun and you possess remarkable beauty.

Aug 9, 2025by Jixiang Niu
Meeting you was the most wonderful surprise of my summer.

When the gentle breeze of early summer blew by, I didn't realize that you would be the reason for my heart to race. These two months of getting along have been like a gentle exploration - your smile, your voice, and those sincere moments that emerged inadvertently have gradually etched into my life.

I originally thought that summer was just the scorching sun and the chirping of cicadas, but you have turned this season into starlight, evening breeze, and the ripples in your eyes when sharing a glass of wine. The moment alcohol melts on the tip of the tongue, it's just like the dizziness when the heart is stirred - even though only a sip has been taken, one can't help but want to stay drunk for a longer time.

Every second spent with you feels like an elongated golden dusk, bright yet reluctant to end. Now, I can finally tell you openly and honestly: "I like you." It's not a momentary impulse but an answer after repeated confirmation. I want to hold your hand and walk through more summers. Also, in every season to come, I wish to turn our story into the longest poem.

Thank you for showing up in my life and turning the music of midsummer into sweet words.
